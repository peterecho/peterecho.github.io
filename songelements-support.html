Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my recordings stored?

All recordings are stored locally on your device. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share them.

How do I export my recordings?

Tap on any recording, then use the share button to export via AirDrop, Messages, Files, or any other app on your device.

Can I recover deleted recordings?

Unfortunately, once a recording is deleted, it cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting important recordings to a backup location.

Does SongElements work offline?

Yes! SongElements works completely offline. No internet connection is required to record, organize, or play back your recordings.
